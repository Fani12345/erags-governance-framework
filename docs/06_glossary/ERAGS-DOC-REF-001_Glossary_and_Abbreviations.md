# Glossary and Abbreviations for ERAGS

## 1. Purpose

This document defines key terms and abbreviations used across the Electrical Roads Asset Governance & Strategy System (ERAGS). It is intended to prevent ambiguity and ensure consistent use of terminology in all documents and models.

## 2. Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Term                                                  | Definition                                                                 |
|--------------|------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| ERAGS        | Electrical Roads Asset Governance & Strategy System        | Governance and strategy system for road-lighting and traffic-signal assets |
| SAMP         | Strategic Asset Management Plan                            | Medium- to long-term plan for the management of asset portfolios         |
| RACI         | Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed              | Responsibility allocation model used in process descriptions              |
| SAP          | Senior Authorised Person                                   | Person formally authorised to issue permits and perform critical switching |
| UPS          | Uninterruptible Power Supply                               | Device providing backup power to critical equipment                       |
| KPI          | Key Performance Indicator                                  | Quantitative measure used to assess performance                           |

## 3. Terms

| Term                     | Definition                                                                                       |
|--------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Asset                    | Any item, thing or component that has potential or actual value to the organisation             |
| Asset Class              | Group of assets with similar characteristics and management needs (e.g. luminaires, controllers)|
| Governance               | Framework of rules, processes and responsibilities used to direct and control asset management  |
| Lifecycle                | Sequence of stages an asset goes through from planning, acquisition, operation, renewal to disposal |
| Availability             | Proportion of time an asset or system is in a state to perform its required function            |
| Reliability              | Probability that an asset performs its required function without failure for a specified period |
| Traceability             | Ability to follow the life of a requirement, decision or data item through its development and use |

## 4. Usage Rules

- All technical and governance documents within ERAGS should use terms as defined here.
- If a document requires a new term, that term should be added to this glossary rather than defined locally, to avoid duplication or conflict.
- Any revision to a term in this glossary must be assessed for impacts on related documents and models.

## 5. Relationships to Other Documents

- All documents listed in `docs/index.md` shall reference this glossary in their metadata.
- Requirements in the ERAGS High-Level Requirements Specification shall use terms defined in this document.
